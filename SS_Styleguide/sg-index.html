Solestruck Styleguide

Below are lists to sections of our styleguide.

Elements

The links in this list are Elements. They make up the base of our styleguide. They consist of type styles, form element styles, buttons, how images are styled, etc. We will use the styles applied to these base level elements and layer them together to make Components which is the list under this one.

  1. Type
  2. Tables
  3. Forms
  4. Buttons
  5. Images

Components

The links in this list are Components. They are groupings of html & css from our Elements. They consist of things like navigation items, responsive embeds, alert bars, and other combinations of elements. We will yet again layer the items in this list together to make Modules which is the list under this one.

  1. Navigation
  2. Labels & Badges
  3. Alerts
  4. Media Object
  5. Responsive Embeds

Modules

The links in this list are Modules. They are groupings of components. They consist of things like headers, footers, sign up forms, product grids, etc. We will yet again layer the items in this list together to make Views which is the end point for this styleguide and is in the list below.

  1. Header
  2. Footer
  3. Product Grid
  4. Campaign Grid
  5. Instagram Grid
  6. Form - Email Check
  7. Form - Sign In
  8. Form - Register
  9. Form - Reset Password
  10. Form - Mailing List
  11. Pagination
  12. Options Panel 1
  13. Product Filter & Sort
  14. Image Slider

Views

The links in this list are Modules. They are groupings of components. They consist of things like headers, footers, sign up forms, product grids, etc. We will yet again layer the items in this list together to make Views which is the end point for this styleguide and is in the list below.

  1. Homepage
  2. Product Listing
  3. Sign In - Email Check
  4. Sign In - Default
  5. Sign In - Register
  6. Sign In - Forgot Password
  7. Sign In - Reset Password
